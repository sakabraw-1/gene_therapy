What is CDKL5 Deficiency Disorder (CDD)?

CDD is a rare brain disorder that affects babies. It causes:

  • Non-stop seizures (epilepsy)
  • Big delays in growing up ‚Äî like not walking or talking
  • It's hard on families, and big drug companies often ignore it because it's rare
