What is CDKL5 Deficiency Disorder?

CDKL5 Deficiency Disorder (CDD) is a rare genetic condition causing severe seizures, developmental delays, and motor impairments. Children with CDD face daily challenges, but gene therapy offers real hope.

Our Mission: Develop AAV9 gene therapy to restore missing CDKL5 protein function and give these children a chance at a better life.
