UPCOMING WEBINAR

Gene Therapy for CDKL5:
The History, Progress & Next Steps

Sunday, October 26, 2025
9:00 - 10:00 AM CDT
Virtual Event (Webinar)

About This Webinar

The US FDA has provided positive feedback + IND suggestions for gene replacement therapy for CDKL5. This is a groundbreaking moment for our community!

Join us for an exclusive webinar where we'll share the latest developments in CDKL5 gene therapy research. This session will cover:

  • FDA feedback and IND pathway for CDKL5 gene therapy
  • Historical timeline of CDKL5 gene therapy development
  • Current progress on definitive preclinical studies
  • Next steps toward first-in-human trials
  • How families can get involved and support the research
  • Q&A session with experts and parent advocates

Featured Speakers

RB

Rachel Bailey, PhD

Lead Researcher

Dr. Bailey is a leading expert in CDKL5 deficiency disorder and has been instrumental in advancing gene therapy research. Her work focuses on developing safe and effective gene replacement strategies for rare neurological conditions.

JJ

Jainu Jogani

Parent Advocate & Community Leader

Jainu is a dedicated parent advocate who has been at the forefront of raising awareness and funds for CDKL5 gene therapy research. Her personal journey and advocacy work have been crucial in building community support and driving progress.

Hosted By

American Society of Gene + Cell Therapy (ASGCT)

ASGCT is the leading professional organization for researchers and clinicians in gene and cell therapy, advancing the field through education, advocacy, and collaboration.

Child's Cure Genetic Research

Child's Cure is dedicated to accelerating genetic research and treatments for rare pediatric diseases, with a focus on innovative therapeutic approaches.
