Sappadu/Sadhya Lunch to Support CDKL5

Come for the meal, stay for the mission.

📅 Sunday, March 22, 2026 🕒 11:30am – 12:30pm 📍 Madras Idli Kadai, Pleasanton, CA 🏠 Hosted by the CDKL5 Community

What began as a simple community gathering turned into a powerful demonstration of what's possible when people come together for a shared mission. The Sappadu/Sadhya Lunch fundraiser on March 22, 2026 is a story worth telling — and a model worth replicating. 💙

How It Came Together

Hosting a local restaurant fundraiser is a simple and meaningful way to support the development of gene therapy for CDKL5. Our community organized a Sappadu/Sadhya Lunch fundraiser, and it was encouraging to see how quickly support came together — from ticket sales to direct donations.

With just a bit of coordination, a welcoming venue, and outreach to friends, family, and the broader community, we were able to successfully meet our fundraising goal. Events like these not only raise critical funds but also help spread awareness about CDKL5.

Why Events Like This Matter

Every dollar raised brings us closer to clinical trials. But beyond the funds, community events like this one do something equally important: they build a network of people who care, who understand the urgency, and who will champion this cause in their own circles.

A restaurant fundraiser requires no lab, no technical expertise — just heart and a community willing to show up. That's exactly what happened here.

Key ingredients of a successful restaurant fundraiser:

  • A welcoming local venue willing to partner with you
  • A clear ticket price and fundraising goal
  • Outreach to friends, family, and the broader community
  • A customized Givebutter fundraising link to track ticket sales and donations
  • Passion and belief in the mission
