What We'll Discuss

Our panel session, "Moving Mountains: Reyna's Story and the Summit Still Ahead for CDKL5 Gene Therapy," will cover:

  • Reyna's Journey: A powerful first-hand account of living with CDKL5 Deficiency Disorder and the hope that gene therapy represents for families
  • Scientific Progress: Updates on the CDKL5 gene therapy research pipeline and what's been achieved so far
  • Clinical Trial Readiness: Insights into the path toward Phase I clinical trials at UCSF
  • The Road Ahead: Challenges, milestones, and what the future holds for CDKL5 gene therapy
  • Community Collaboration: How families, researchers, and clinicians work together to advance rare disease treatments
