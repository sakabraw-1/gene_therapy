About the Event

TriValley Melodies is proud to present UMEED! — a Bollywood musical fundraiser featuring Bay Area’s top singers. “Umeed” means hope in Hindi and Urdu, and that’s exactly what this evening is about.

Every ticket purchased and every dollar raised goes directly toward funding CDKL5 gene therapy clinical trials — giving hope to children and families affected by CDKL5 Deficiency Disorder.

The evening includes live Bollywood music performances, an Open Mic hour, and a chance to connect with the CDKL5 community. Come for the music, stay for the mission.
