🔬 Internship Opportunity

We are inviting motivated interns, age 14 and above, to join our 7-week virtual internship from June 1 to July 17 and contribute to meaningful CDKL5 projects across small molecule, gene therapy, and mRNA-based therapy.

This is a part-time, unpaid opportunity, but interns will receive full credit for their contributions, including recognition in publications where applicable. There is also a possibility of extension beyond the initial internship period.

This is a great opportunity to work alongside industry leaders, gain hands-on exposure to real-world biotech research, build a strong foundation in a cutting-edge field, and solve real world problems.

How to Apply

Email support@cdkl5genetherapy.com with your resume and a brief note of interest. The interview process includes an initial screening, followed by two technical interviews.
