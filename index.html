How AAV9 Delivers Hope for Brain Health

AAV9 (Adeno-Associated Virus serotype 9) acts like a tiny delivery boat, carrying a healthy CDKL5 gene to brain cells. Once it reaches the neurons, this gene starts producing a protein that helps grow brain connections and keeps signals steady. This innovative approach offers hope for treating conditions linked to CDKL5 deficiencies, such as developmental and neurological challenges, paving the way for better lives.
