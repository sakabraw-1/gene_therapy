Partner with local restaurants

A fun way to fundraise is to partner with a restaurant – who doesn't like eating and raising money for great causes!? There are different ways they can help...

Host a lunch or dinner

Work with the restaurant for a date and time to invite friends and family to dine together. The restaurant will donate a portion of the proceeds from your event to our cause.

'Dine to donate' events

Coordinate a specific day where a portion of all the proceeds made that day go to The CDKL5 Foundation. Promote your event within your community!

Request a donation

Request a donation from the restaurant. It could be "in-kind," which could be in the form of gift cards for you to use in a raffle. Or the restaurant can make a direct monetary donation to our foundation.
